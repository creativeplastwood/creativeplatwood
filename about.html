About Us

We are highly professionals in the industry of WPC (Wood Plastic Composite).We are leading manufacturer of quality WPC products likewise Doors, Windows, Door Frames, Window Frames, Door shutters, Window shutters, Panel Doors, Flush Doors, Modular Shutters and many more. We have occupied approximately 20,000 square feet of land for our manufacturing unit at Kamla Amrut Industrial Park, Indrad (Kadi) near Chhatral. In the process of manufacturing, Our team strictly observe to spot any defects in terms of quality. Offering high quality and effective customer services come at very top in our list of priorities. Our products are manufactured by imported advanced extruder machinery. We try to offer our level best by following indian standards and by best manufacturing technologies.
We have introduced our new endeavour in WPC industry that does not mean we are new in the industry because we have been leading manufacturer of all types of wooden products since 1980. In this long course, we have realised that the excessive usage of wooden has adverse effects on environment as we all are being victim of global warming.
Additionally it remains the fact that WPC products are environment friendly since the amount of wooden used in our products are relatively less and at the same time, they are recyclable as well. By manufacturing WPC products we understand our obligation towards the nature and try to fulfil it comprehensively.
